# Liberation Plugin
Add The Liberators to the game!

## What are The Liberators?
This team is a an unofficial faction.
In their lore they try to help SCPs. Help them in a way, so they can contribute to society in a good way.
Try to make the SCPs good and not bad killing machines.

## In game
They spawn early in the round.
Their goal is to arrest as many people as possible without using lethal force.
Using their Stun Gun, they can "stun" anyone shot. This means, that they can pretty easily arrest anyone.
When escorting a player to surface zone, they become part of The Liberators AND all SCPs get bonus Hume Shield.
